About Us

Our aim is to provide a platform where medical records are easily shared and accessed by both patients and the medical fraternity. A doctor can easily view previous interactions the patient has had with the healthcare system and update patient records about the current visit in an easy way. The patient also has access to the enterity of their medical history and have full autonomy to authorize access as well as delete all their records should they choose to.
